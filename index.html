About Me

Who Am I?

I'm a 3rd year Computer Science Honors student at University of Manitoba with a curiosity for learning how things work.

My journey in technology began in 7th grade when I found out that softwares aren't built with magic spells, back then I started by classic old youtube. Built some super weird games and websites which made no sense, but it was fun. Since then, I've been dedicated to expanding my knowledge and skills in various things CS has to offer, with particular interests in Computer Vision, Embedded Systems and Software dev .

When I'm not coding, you can find me sketching, watching documentaries, reading biographies and watching anime. Also I do find peace in web dev, trying to create random effects from scratch and learn how the languages work. Apart from that I'm an aspiring Intrapreneur, like the idea of knowledge of completely unrelated fields to solve a problem .
